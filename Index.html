About Us

Kastilyos Kitchen

Founded in 2025 by Jennifer C. Castillo, Kastilyos Kitchen began with a love for authentic Filipino cooking and has grown into a trusted catering service in Antipolo City. We specialize in bringing people together through food, whether it’s a wedding, birthday, corporate event, or family celebration. Every dish is prepared from scratch, served with care, and designed to leave guests delighted and satisfied.
