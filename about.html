Kastilyos Kitchen

Business Story

Founded in 2025 by Jennifer C. Castillo, Kastilyos Kitchen began with a love for authentic Filipino cooking and has grown into a trusted catering service in Antipolo City. We specialize in bringing people together through food, whether it's a wedding, birthday, corporate event, or family celebration. Every dish is prepared from scratch, served with care, and designed to leave guests delighted and satisfied.

Mission

To serve heartfelt Filipino-inspired catering with fresh ingredients, dependable service, and beautifully prepared dishes for every occasion.

Vision

To be a trusted catering choice in Antipolo City, known for elegant presentations, flavorful food, and memorable guest experiences.

Why Choose Us

We focus on scratch-made food, warm hospitality, and thoughtful details so every celebration feels smooth, special, and welcoming.
